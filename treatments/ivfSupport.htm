IVF Support

IVF

Acupuncture and Herbal medicine support before an embryo transfer and after embryo transfer and Acupuncture can help maintain the pregnancy after embryo has been implanted.

Most female patients are not ready for IVF transfer, and this is why there are failure and miscarriage.

Acupuncture treatment recommended prior to 3 to 4 month. This preparation increase follicle size, egg quality and greatly lessens risk of IVF failure by balancing hormone (progesterone & estrogen) levels, regulating period cycle and increasing blood circulation to uterine and ovary. This IVF support enhances more chance to conceive successfully.

After transfer embryos, it is important to assist blood circulation in the uterus, nourish the embryo growth and relaxing the uterus and mind to prevent uterine contraction that cause bleeding and miscarriage.

How does it work?

Acupuncture and Herbal medicine enhance reproductive system by:

*Increasing uterine blood circulation

*Regulating hormone level

*Remove blockage and stasis

*Increasing basal body temperature

*Enhance free flow of Qi and blood

*Regulate period and reduce pain

*Assist the ovulation

*Increase thickness lining of the uterus

*Help unblock fallopian tubes

Diagnosis

Analyze body’s individual condition from a TCM perspective, including past and current sign and symptoms, pulse and tongue diagnosis and abdominal palpation. FSH levels, hormone levels, cyclic temperature chart menstrual regularity, and quality of the menstrual blood. The combination of all of this can determine the treatment.
